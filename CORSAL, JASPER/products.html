TOKNENENG

We've elevated the classic Filipino street food tokneneng into a signature delicacy. We start with carefully selected quail eggs, which we coat in our own specially crafted batter, infused with just the right blend of flour, annatto, and aromatic spices. Each egg is fried to golden perfection, offering a crispy exterior that contrasts beautifully with the soft, tender center. Our secret? A vibrant orange batter that not only adds color but layers of flavor. Paired with our house-made vinegar dipping sauce, enriched with garlic and chili, this creation is a small but unforgettable taste of tradition, reimagined for the modern palate.

SQUIDBALL

Indulge in the delightful experience of our squid balls, a culinary masterpiece that captures the essence of the ocean in every bite. Served with an array of delectable dipping sauces, such as a tangy soy-vinegar blend or a creamy wasabi mayo, these squid balls are not just a snack; they are an adventure for the senses, perfect for sharing at gatherings or enjoying as a satisfying treat any time of day.

HILAW NA MANGGA

Experience the refreshing taste of hilaw na mangga, a beloved Filipino delicacy served with a side of bagoong (fermented shrimp paste). This classic appetizer features crisp, green mango slices that offer a delightful balance of tanginess and sweetness. The crunchy texture of the mango, paired with the savory, salty notes of bagoong, creates a unique flavor combination that is both invigorating and satisfying. Perfect for sharing or enjoying on your own, hilaw na mangga is a must-try for anyone looking to explore the vibrant flavors of Filipino cuisine at Mama Imelda's Kitchen!
