TOKNENENG

We've elevated the classic Filipino street food tokneneng into a signature delicacy. We start with carefully selected quail eggs, which we coat in our own specially crafted batter, infused with just the right blend of flour, annatto, and aromatic spices. Each egg is fried to golden perfection, offering a crispy exterior that contrasts beautifully with the soft, tender center. Our secret? A vibrant orange batter that not only adds color but layers of flavor. Paired with our house-made vinegar dipping sauce, enriched with garlic and chili, this creation is a small but unforgettable taste of tradition, reimagined for the modern palate.

FISHBALL

Our premium fishball is fished freshly, locally, and meticously chosen at the local palengke. Each Flat disc of balls are fried to perfection by the golden oil from the foreheads of the makers. Served with only the finest soy sauce of the Chinese and chili garlic from the Russians. When eaten, will give you the best taste you've had in your entire life.

CHICKENBALL

Introducing our exquisite Chicken Balls—tender morsels of premium chicken, expertly seasoned and crafted into delightful bites. Each ball is fried to golden perfection, creating a crispy exterior that gives way to a juicy, flavorful center. Paired with a luscious dipping sauce that harmonizes the richness of the chicken with zesty notes, these chicken balls offer a sophisticated twist on a beloved classic, making every bite an irresistible treat for the senses. Perfect for sharing—or not!

SQUIDBALL

Indulge in the delightful experience of our squid balls, a culinary masterpiece that captures the essence of the ocean in every bite. Expertly crafted from the freshest squid, each ball is blended with a harmonious mix of savory seasonings and aromatic herbs, creating a taste sensation that is both delicate and bold. The squid is finely minced and combined with a touch of garlic, ginger, and hints of citrus, infusing the mixture with a burst of flavor that dances on your palate. Lightly battered and expertly fried to achieve a perfect golden-brown crisp, our squid balls offer an enticing crunch that gives way to a tender, juicy interior. Served with an array of delectable dipping sauces, such as a tangy soy-vinegar blend or a creamy wasabi mayo, these squid balls are not just a snack; they are an adventure for the senses, perfect for sharing at gatherings or enjoying as a satisfying treat any time of day.

TURON

Sink your teeth into our heavenly Turon—a delightful treat that redefines sweetness! Each golden, crispy roll is lovingly wrapped around ripe bananas and a sprinkle of caramelized brown sugar, then fried to a perfect crunch. With every bite, you’ll experience the satisfying crackle of the exterior giving way to the warm, gooey interior that makes your taste buds dance with joy. Whether enjoyed as a snack or dessert, this classic Filipino favorite is so good that you might find yourself dreaming of it long after the last bite. Trust us, once you’ve tasted our Turon, ordinary desserts will seem like a distant memory!

About Us

Introducing Mama Imelda and Jasper

Welcome to Mama Imelda's Kitchen, where tradition meets taste! My name is Jasper, and I proudly assist in promoting our beloved family venture. Our kitchen is a celebration of culinary heritage, lovingly created by my mom, Mama Imelda. With her rich recipes passed down through generations, she brings the authentic flavors of our culture to life, ensuring every dish tells a story.

At Mama Imelda's Kitchen, our mission is to provide a warm and welcoming dining experience that feels like home. We strive to serve delicious, quality meals using fresh, locally sourced ingredients. My mom’s passion for cooking inspires us to maintain high standards while embracing the spirit of family and community. We believe that food is not just about sustenance but a way to connect people and create lasting memories.

Together, our vision is to expand Mama Imelda's Kitchen into a beloved dining destination where everyone can gather to enjoy hearty meals and cherished moments. As I focus on advertising and reaching out to our community, we hope to share our culinary delights and the love behind each dish, inviting you to experience the warmth of our kitchen firsthand.
